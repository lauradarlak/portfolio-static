Laura Darlak

Hi there! I am a WordPress Developer and practitioner of simple living. I started developing custom WordPress themes in 2017 after leaving a career in applied gerontological research. I have since graduated from the Flatiron School’s Full Stack Web Developer Program and work at a fast-paced web development agency.

I am interested in creating organizational culture that supports team members in actualizing their most fulfilling work and personal life. What I enjoy most about Web Development is the opportunity to use my technical knowledge in service to those who are committed to creating the greatest good for all.
