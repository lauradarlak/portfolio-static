The Mentoring Alliance

Project Details

  • premedadvisor.com

The Mentoring Alliance is a team of medical school admissions counselors, offering individualized advising to support students through the admissions process. The growing consulting business wanted to build a reputable advice blog and online subscriber list to pave the way for plans to build an online business by offering educational content and workshops through their website.

Polypore Design designed and developed a custom WordPress theme that will support member-only content. We developed a search optimized blog with automated email marketing integration to help the client build an online audience. The client is able to easily modify website content and publish blog posts using the WordPress content management system (CMS).
